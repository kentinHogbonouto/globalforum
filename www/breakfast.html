Breakfast Briefings

Welcome to the Global Forum Cities—an influential global conference positioned at the crossroads of business and policy, dedicated to advancing urban excellence. This exceptional event serves as a nexus for public and private sector entities, startups, and investors, all driven by a shared mission: fostering collaborative dialogues to drive and shape the future of urban landscapes. Hosted in iconic cities including London, Paris, New York, and Singapore — the forum brings together business leaders and policymakers 4 times a year.

At the heart of the Global Forum Cities lies the world's most influential series of summits focused on urban progress.The Global Forum Cities offers two pathways: ‘Breakfast Briefing Sessions' and our 'Global Summit’. Attendees have the choice to engage in one or both events according to their preferences and requirements. Drawing together luminaries from 20 diverse sectors, these discussions delve into the myriad of opportunities and challenges that 21st-century cities face. As a dynamic platform, the forum serves as an arena where experts collaboratively shape the urban agenda, nurturing ideas and partnerships to drive the evolution of cities into adaptive, vibrant, and sustainable centres of growth.
